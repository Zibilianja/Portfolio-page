Intro

My name is Jonathan and I am from Denver Colorado and moved back here from Portland Oregon in 2013. As most Coloradans I have a passion for the outdoors. As such, there is no better place to be. On my free time you can find me rock climbing, hiking, fly-fishing, biking... essentially, you're likely find me outside.

About Me

My first career and still a passion of mine is in mental health therapy. I got my Master's degree in Clinical Mental Health Counseling in 2018 and worked in my private therapy practice since then. In that process I have worked with many people and over time, I discovered that I didn't want therapy to be my primary source of income. I intend one day to get a small private practice back on the side, but I didn't like how the pressure of having enough clients to make a stable income impacted the work that I did with people.

Because of my studies in psychology and work experience, I have developed a lot of my communication skills and am quite effective at working with other people. I know how to take the lead when necessary and also how to step back and follow. I know how to effectively listen and ask the right questions. I am excited to see how these skills might apply to the tech world.

I have always had a passion for technology and a deep fascination with how it all works. 2020 was a year of intimate reflection for all of us and for me, it helped me see that I wanted to pursue that passion for technology and learn to code. I began to teach myself through programs like FreeCodeCamp.org, TheOdinProject, Udemy courses in Python and Javascript. I discovered that I enjoyed coding a whole lot and so I began looking for programs to really solidify my skills and pursue this as a career. I began the flex program with Thinkful bootcamp in June of 2021.
